CUBOID.

Welcome to Cuboid, where intuitive tools like Cuboid Read, Explore, and Share transform your reading journey, making it more engaging and connected.

Since 2024, Cuboid's mission is to make reading engaging with Cuboid Read, Explore, and Share—offering personalized, interactive, and connected literary experiences.

Reading isn’t always easy. It requires time, effort, and focus. But when you’re lost in a book, it’s incredibly rewarding.

We feel the same way about creating Cuboid. Our passion for books and the readers we serve is the driving force behind everything we do.

We are a global community of readers from over 30 countries, with connections spanning Berlin, Los Angeles, Tokyo, and beyond.

We are a team of passionate readers, writers, and literary enthusiasts, many of whom use Cuboid daily. Our diverse backgrounds range from academics to self-taught experts, united by a shared belief: that each of us has the skills and knowledge to contribute to something bigger—shaping the future of reading and literary culture.

We’re passionate about the books we read, but we’re equally dedicated to growing and improving as readers and individuals.

We strive to create a space where readers can grow both personally and intellectually, offering numerous opportunities to learn from and with each other.

In addition to our learning resources, we support continuous growth through book discussions, reading groups, and literary workshops, where you can explore new genres, share insights, and enhance your reading experience.
